SYNTAX.
Lino ly'essomo ly'ebyennimi ery'ekenneenya ensimba y'emboozi, amakulu gaazo n'amateeka agagobererwa mu kuzimba emboozi. (Syntax is the study of the formation of sentences, how words are combined to larger units than words, to phrases and sentences that are well-formed strings in a given language)

[SUBSECTION] Empeerezi esooka
Zino mu luganda ziri ssatu , A,E,O, ziyinza okubaawo oba obutabaawo.
Empeerezi esooka okugifuna livinzira ku mpeerezi y'ennyingo y'olubu lw'erinnya eryo.
Empeerezi y'ennyingo y'olubu bweba "a" n'emepeerezi esooka eba "a" - "a"-"a" okugeza abawala , akatundu , abasajja.
Empeerezi y'ennyingo y'olubu bweba i oba obutabaawo empeerezi esooka eba "e"-"i-e" nga ekikopo , ekisero , eryato , ekyuma , ekyalo.
Amannya amala ag'olubu 5Li agatandiika n'ensirifu ezaanabansaana nago gatandisa empeerezi esooka "e" okugeza ettama kiya mu
Eddiba, ezadde, eggolu - eliwolu.
Amannya agayingira mu lubu lw'erinnya 9N ne 10N nago galina empeerezi esooka "e" newankubadde tegalina nnyingo y'empeerezi erabika.
Ente -e-n-te , embusi,- E-n-buzi , Entebbe, enswa.
Amannya amasake agava mu nnimi endala gatandika n'empeerezi "e" kuba gaba mu mbu za 5Li , 9N, 10N era gaba n'ensirifu ezaanabansasaana okugeza; essati , ebbookisi, essowaani , ekkalaamu, eppeesa.
Empeerezi y'ennyingo y'olubu bwebeera "u" empeerezi esooka eba "o"-"u" okugeza ; omusota , omusomesa , okusomesa , olubaawo, omulenzi , omukyala n'ebirala.
Errinya eriri mu IMU bwe litaba na nnyingo ya lubu terbia na mpeerezi esooka okugeza; kasooli , lumonde , lubaale , kawumpuli , bbiiya, muwogo.
Erinnya bwe liba eryenkalakalira terbia na mpeerezi esooka okugeza Ssentumbwe, Nakalembe, Kajugira, Naddumba , Nabiccu , Nakandi nebirala.
